Get to Know Me

Hello, I'm Sarah

Welcome to my little corner of the internet! I'm a 28-year-old writer, traveler, and coffee enthusiast living in Portland, Oregon. This blog is where I share my adventures, thoughts, and the little moments that make life beautiful.

When I'm not writing, you can find me exploring local coffee shops, hiking in the Pacific Northwest, or curled up with a good book and my cat, Luna. I believe in the power of storytelling to connect us all, and I hope my words resonate with you in some way.

Thank you for being here and being part of this journey with me. Every story is better when shared with friends, and that's exactly what I hope we become.
